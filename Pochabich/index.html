Pochabych

Pochabych (rus. "Похабыч", expanted form of "старик Похабыч") - a playful term for someone who constantly searches for a double (figurative), often not entirely proper, meaning in another person's words.

Furthermore, "Old Man Pokhabych" (rus. "старик Похабыч") is a reference to the Soviet writer Lazar Lagin's fairy tale "Old Man Hottabych".

Which also contains a duality!
